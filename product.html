ONE-ON-ONE

Programming Education

Since 2013, we’ve been offering the only personalized one-on-one programming lessons in Japan, led by dedicated instructors. With courses in AI, web design, and career transitions into engineering, we provide direct guidance to help individuals land fulfilling tech careers. We don’t just teach programming—we aim to change lives. Whether you want to build your IT skills, become an engineer, or develop an app, we’re here to make that dream come true.
We offer programs for students, women, and people with disabilities, delivering high-quality education for all to help eliminate poverty and inequality through IT.

MEDIA

Programming Learning Platform

Founded in 2015, the "SocialTech Blog" has grown to become Japan’s largest programming education platform with over 6 million monthly page views. We’ve also launched a YouTube channel to offer free, beginner-friendly programming tutorials. Whether you're looking to self-study or fast-track your career change, we provide tailored learning resources through articles and video content to meet a variety of needs.

CAREER

Career Development Services

We support career transitions from non-technical fields into engineering, guiding individuals through every step. From resume and portfolio reviews to interview coaching, we help candidates secure positions in tech. We’re here to align your personal strengths and goals with the best opportunities, ensuring you feel growth and success throughout the job search process.

B2B

Corporate Training Programs

We offer tailored corporate programming training programs to enhance employee skills, support certification achievements, and improve company welfare. With our experienced engineers, we can customize programs to fit your business model, helping you develop in-house IT talent and adapt to an evolving market. Our training helps businesses drive sustainable growth through innovation.
