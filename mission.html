Achieving the S2DGs

Among the 169 targets under the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), SocialTech has set five key goals that we are committed to achieving.

01

End Poverty

We aim to help more people break free from relative poverty by supporting them in becoming high-earning engineers. We also provide IT education to children from underprivileged backgrounds to stop the cycle of poverty.

02

Quality Education for All

手We provide affordable, high-quality technical and vocational education, aiming to create a platform where anyone can become an engineer. We also offer STEAM education for children, fostering globally valuable talents for the future.

03

Achieve Gender Equality

We believe that increasing the number of female engineers can impact gender equality in the economic and educational sectors. By empowering women, we aim to create more opportunities for women in the workforce and support single mothers.

04

Productive Employment for All

For long-term economic growth, it’s essential to expand productive industries and develop talent. Engineers are a key part of driving innovation, and we believe their work creates fulfilling and meaningful employment opportunities.

05

Reduce Inequality

Engineers with disabilities can also work productively. We aim to close the wage gap and increase the percentage of full-time employees with disabilities by offering everyone the chance to become an engineer.
