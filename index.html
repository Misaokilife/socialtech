WORLD PEACE

Achieving world peace through IT education

MISSION

Realizing World Peace

The ultimate goal of humanity is world peace. To solve the many societal issues that threaten global peace, innovation is essential. SocialTech aims to tackle this grand theme by nurturing innovators through programming education.

Achieving the S2DGs

Among the 17 global goals set by the United Nations for Sustainable Development (SDGs), SocialTech is committed to realizing five specific goals through our services.

PRODUCT

MAN TO MAN

Programming Education

Since 2013, SocialTech has been offering one-on-one programming lessons with dedicated instructors, the only such service in Japan. AI/Artificial Intelligence and web design courses are also available.

CAREER

Career Introduction Service

We provide advice on resumes and job history for those looking to transition into engineering roles, and offer interview preparation support. We help clients choose the optimal career path from a wealth of job opportunities.

MEDIA

Programming Learning Media

We operate "SocialTech Blog," one of Japan's largest programming learning platforms, read by over 2 million people monthly, and also share learning information on our YouTube channel.

B2B

Corporate Training Service

We offer corporate programming training tailored to your business model, focusing on employee skill development, certification acquisition, and welfare improvement.

ABOUT US

Three Organizational Cultures

01 FLEXIBLE
We promote flexible work styles, such as remote work, full flex time, and side jobs, to ensure everyone can perform at their best.
02 TEAL
We aim to function as a single, interconnected entity, operating as a flat organization where departments and teams collaborate seamlessly.
03 SELF REALIZATION
We set OKRs at the company, department, and individual levels every quarter to align personal career goals with the company's direction.

VISION
